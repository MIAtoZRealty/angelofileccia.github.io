Data Deletion Instructions — Angelo Fileccia Real Estate Content Application

Effective date: 2026-05-16

What data this app stores

The Angelo Fileccia Real Estate Content Application stores only:

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens issued by connected social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Google Business Profile)
  • Content drafts and metadata related to scheduled posts by the account owner

The App does not collect data from external users. It is operated solely by Angelo Fileccia to publish to his own connected accounts.

How to request deletion

To request deletion of all data the App holds:

  1. Email angelo.fileccia@gmail.com with the subject line "Data deletion request"
  2. Or revoke the App's access via the connected platform's app permissions settings (the most immediate action — removes the OAuth token's ability to access any data)

All requests are honored within seven (7) days. Confirmation will be sent to the requesting email address.
